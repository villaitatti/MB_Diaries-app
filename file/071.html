Saturday, April 29, 1933, I Tatti

Logan

Still cold and grey

Miss Blair from the Toledo (N. S. A.) Museum called to see the Chinese things.

Daniel (Sir Augustus) came to tea and we chatted pleasantly

Sunday, April 30, 1933, I Tatti

Still cold and grey rain

Little Gianni Origo died this morning. Poor Iris!

We had Placci and Mrs. Cooper Hewitt to lunch. Their talk was awful, his backstains gossip, his boasting about Italic efficiency. Mrs. Shapley and her 2 daughters came to tea and I took them to see the Casa Boccaccio.
