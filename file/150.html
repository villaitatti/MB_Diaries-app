Sunday, October 1, 1933, I Tatti

Still scirocco

A quiet enjoyable day of work, but I dined at Poggio with Lina and Aubrey. O I forgot Vavala and the Shapleys to lunch. B.B. and Nicky at Venice. Willy Scull to tea.
